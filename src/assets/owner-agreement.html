Property Management Agreement

Agreement Overview

THIS AGREEMENT is made and entered into by and between {{organization-office}} ("AvenueWest" / "Agent"), a corporation organized under the laws of the State of {{organizationState}}, having its principal office at {{accountingOfficeAddress}}, and {{ownerFullName}} ("Owner"), of {{ownerAddress}}.

Property: {{propertyAddress}} ({{propertyCode}})

RECITALS

a) Owner holds title and is the owner of the above-described real property.

b) Agent is in the business of operating and managing real estate similar to the above-described property.

c) Owner desires to engage Agent for professional rental management services and Agent desires to provide such services under this agreement.

SECTION ONE - EMPLOYMENT OF AGENT

1.1 Agent shall act as the exclusive agent of Owner to manage, lease, operate and maintain the property.

1.2 This agreement shall be for a term of 12 months commencing on {{agreementStartDate}}.

1.3 This agreement will continue until terminated in accordance with Section 13.

1.4 Owner return: {{ownerRevenueSplitPercent}}% of adjusted gross rents; Agent share: {{officeRevenueSplitPercent}}%. Adjusted gross rents are gross rents less applicable taxes, referral fees, maid service fees and other direct booking costs.

Agent will target an adjusted gross rent of ${{adjustedGrossRentTarget}} monthly.

SECTION TWO - AGENT DUTIES

2.1 Agent shall inspect the property, recommend changes, and undertake approved improvements. Missing inventory may be supplied and charged to Owner.

2.2 Agent shall make reasonable efforts to lease the property and may negotiate, execute, renew, and extend lease/rental tenancies on behalf of Owner.

2.3 Agent shall use good faith and due diligence to achieve the highest rental income while preserving the property long-term, including authority to set rates, deposits, discounts, and rental business terms.

2.4 Agent shall deposit rental receipts in a trust account in compliance with Colorado Real Estate Commission rules until monthly disbursements are made.

2.5 Agent shall provide managerial and housekeeping services for the property.

SECTION THREE - OWNER DUTIES

3.1 Owner shall keep the property in first-class structural condition and furnished in a manner acceptable to Agent.

3.2 Owner shall provide required inventory and fund periodic replacement/refresh as needed.

3.3 Owner shall provide, at Owner expense, high-speed internet, cable/streaming, electric/gas, water, sewer, and trash services.

3.4 Owner shall timely pay all ownership costs, including debt service, HOA assessments, and amenity fees.

3.5 Owner shall pay for seasonal cleanings (including carpet cleaning and touch-up painting) and approved subcontractor work charged to Owner account.

3.6 Owner agrees not to enter, or permit others to enter, the property without prior Agent approval while this agreement is active.

SECTION FOUR - ADVERTISING AND PROMOTION

4.1 Agent shall advertise vacancies through reasonable channels, including web exposure.

4.2 Agent shall design and distribute promotional materials at Agent expense.

4.3 Primary marketing is through relationships with corporations, relocation companies, and corporate housing agencies.

SECTION FIVE - MAINTENANCE, REPAIRS AND OPERATIONS

5.1 Agent shall use best efforts to maintain the property and coordinate utilities/services required for operation.

5.2 For non-emergency items over $200.00, Agent will make reasonable effort to contact Owner. Emergency/protective expenditures may proceed without prior notice.

5.3 If critical appliances/fixtures fail, Agent may repair/replace on Owner behalf where delay could materially impact occupancy/revenue.

5.4 In-house maintenance labor fee: ${{inHouseMaintenanceHourlyFee}} per hour.

5.5 Subcontracted maintenance is passed through to Owner with {{subcontractedMaintenanceMarkupPercent}}% markup.

SECTION SIX - REIMBURSEMENTS

6.1 Costs resulting from this agreement (except costs expressly assumed by Agent) are Owner responsibility. If rents are insufficient, Owner shall reimburse Agent within 15 days of demand.

SECTION SEVEN - GOVERNMENT REGULATIONS

7.1 Agent shall manage property in compliance with applicable federal, state, county and municipal laws.

SECTION EIGHT - INSURANCE

8.1 Owner shall maintain comprehensive general liability insurance with limits not less than ${{ownerLiabilityInsuranceMinimum}} per occurrence.

8.2 Owner is recommended to maintain fire/casualty, loss of rents, and personal property coverage (individually or through HOA as applicable).

8.3 Owner shall name Agent as additional insured and provide insurance certificates as requested.

SECTION NINE - COLLECTION OF INCOME; LEGAL ACTION

9.1 Agent shall use best efforts to collect rents and other income; collection is not guaranteed.

9.2 Agent shall use good faith efforts to collect unpaid rent/damages via deposits, cards, demand letters, or negotiations.

9.3 Agent may execute notices/demands and pursue lawful collections/remedies in Owner name as needed.

SECTION TEN - BANK ACCOUNTS

10.1 Agent shall deposit property revenues into Agent trust account, without commingling, and disburse net balances to Owner after authorized deductions.

SECTION ELEVEN - RECORDS AND REPORTS

11.1 Agent shall maintain books and records for property operations, available for Owner review by appointment with at least three days notice.

11.2 Agent shall provide monthly statement of revenues/expenditures and concession summary.

11.3 Agent shall provide annual summary within 30 days after calendar year end.

SECTION TWELVE - ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RIGHTS OF AGENT

12.1 Agent shall perform services necessary for proper operation and management, and report material conditions to Owner.

12.2 Agent uses reasonable diligence to correct issues but does not guarantee repairs in all circumstances nor guarantee against all income loss/damage events.

12.3 Agent is not obligated to advance funds. If advanced, Owner shall repay on demand; unpaid balances may accrue interest up to 24% annual plus collection costs as permitted by law.

12.4 Agent may terminate if Owner fails to cure reasonable property conditions required for proper management after 30 days.

SECTION THIRTEEN - TERMINATION AND RENEWAL

13.1 Agreement renews annually unless terminated per these terms. After initial one-year term, either party may terminate with 90 days written notice; shorter notice may be accepted if unit is vacant with no pending leases.

13.2 Where tenant occupancy extends beyond notice period, termination occurs at lease end under agreement terms.

SECTION FOURTEEN - SALE OF PROPERTY / ACCESS

14.1 Owner remains obligated to honor binding reservations made by Agent.

14.2 Owner shall promptly notify Agent of any potential sale/listing of the property.

14.3 Voluntary sale terminates agreement upon departure of current tenant or lease end. Contract termination fee due to sale: ${{saleTerminationFee}}.

SECTION FIFTEEN - SUMMARY OF FEES

15.1 Maintenance fees apply to normal wear-and-tear work at ${{inHouseMaintenanceHourlyFee}}/hour plus item costs.

15.2 Annual maintenance includes preventative services (filters, batteries, carpet cleaning, etc.).

15.3 Property repairs are charged to tenant where negligent; otherwise to Owner.

15.4 Online fee (Rent Ready processing): ${{onlineRentReadyFee}}.

15.5 Online clean (if needed): ${{onlineCleanHourlyFee}}/hour.

15.6 Annual linen & towel service: {{annualLinenServiceSelection}}

15.7 Working balance / utility allowance maintained in escrow: ${{workingBalanceEscrowAmount}}.

15.8 Unit furnishing responsibility and furnishing selections: {{furnishingSelectionSummary}}

15.9 Off-line fee (unit breakdown): ${{offlineFee}}.

15.10 Monthly rent disbursement and deduction logic applies as detailed in this agreement.

15.11 Owner payments are processed by direct deposit (next business day target).

15.12 Certain tenant fees may be collected by broker (e.g., pet cleaning, departure, late fees).

SECTION SIXTEEN - FOREIGN OWNERSHIP

16.1 Owner acknowledges U.S. tax withholding rules may apply to foreign ownership and agrees to provide required documentation. Without qualifying documentation, Agent may withhold 30% of applicable gross unit revenue per U.S. tax law.

SECTION SEVENTEEN - INDEMNITY

17.1 Owner indemnifies Agent and affiliates against liabilities/claims arising from Owner-side obligations, including reasonable attorney fees/costs, as permitted by law.

17.2 Agent indemnifies Owner for losses resulting from Agent negligent acts/omissions while performing under this agreement.

SECTION EIGHTEEN - MISCELLANEOUS

18.1 Agent is an independent contractor.

18.2 Completed W-9 required before owner payment.

18.3 Notice requirements, address updates, and delivery standards apply as detailed in this agreement.

18.4 Agreement binds successors/assigns subject to assignment restrictions.

18.5 If any provision is invalid, remaining provisions remain in effect.

18.6 Colorado law governs validity and interpretation.

18.7 This agreement is the complete understanding unless modified in writing and signed.

18.8 Disputes are subject to binding arbitration in Denver, Colorado under AAA rules.

18.9 Prevailing party in enforcement action may recover costs and reasonable attorney fees.

18.10 Modifications require signed written amendment.
